Characters

Since there's 30 characters in this book, I'm not going to name all, but the most important characters are: Ms. Gorf, Ms. Jewls, and Yard Teacher Louis.


Ms. Gorf was a mean teacher who turned kids into apples when they Misbehaved. She ends up being turned into an apple and Yard Teacher Louis accidentaly eats her.


Ms. Jewls is a kind teacher who loved kids and is usually really nice. She stays the same throughout the story.


Yard Teacher Louis is a pretty average yard teacher. He stays the same throughout the story.

Setting

The setting of this story is a 30 story building (which is missing the 19th floor). Some chapters also take place outside in the playarea.


The setting affects the characters by making them have to run/walk, therefore making them tired.

Plot

The plot of the story is kids go to a school that is 30 Stories tall and see crazy things like teachers who turn kids into apples and teachers who dont even exist.


The conflict inside this story is man vs man conflict.


There isn't really a hero or a villian in this story considering there's 30 characters.

Theme

The theme of this story is not making sense. Since that is what the story talks about a lot, that is probably the theme.

Review

I think this book did plot pretty well. I think it could do conflict a bit better. I rate this book 4/5 hats.
